Francisco Castro

I'm currently a post-doctoral research scientist at Uber and I'll be joining UCLA Anderson School of Management as an assistant professor next year. My work focuses on the operations and design of markets with emphasis on their spatial and incentive aspects. I'm particularly interested in the design of optimal pricing policies and contracts that can be used in digital marketplaces such as ride-hailing platforms and ad exchanges.
